About the Medication Tracker

This application is built to empower users to track and manage their medications and related symptoms with confidence. Whether you are on a complex treatment plan or tracking simple prescriptions, this tool brings clarity and control to your health management.

  • Simple and intuitive medication logging
  • Track side effects and symptoms easily
  • Review your complete medication history in one place

Side Effect Logging

Monitor how medications affect your body by logging symptoms like fatigue, nausea, and more. Each log is timestamped and linked to specific drugs, making it easy to review or share with your physician.

Personalized Health Records

Your profile stores demographic info like age, gender, and country to help tailor insights and maintain continuity of care. View your full medication history at a glance — all safely stored under your account.

Peace of Mind

Never miss a dose. With structured entries and simplified UI, this tool removes complexity and helps you stay on schedule — reducing the risk of human error in managing prescriptions.
